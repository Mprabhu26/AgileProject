How to Apply for Projects

Browse available projects that match your skills and interests. When you find a project you'd like to join:

  1. Review the requirements - Check if your skills match the project needs
  2. Check the timeline - Make sure you're available during the project dates
  3. Click "Apply" - Submit your interest to the Project Manager
  4. Wait for review - Your application will be reviewed by the Resource Manager

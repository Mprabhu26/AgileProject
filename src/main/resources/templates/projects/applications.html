How to Review Applications

As a Project Manager, you should review each application carefully:

  • Check skills match - Compare employee skills with project requirements
  • Review availability - Ensure the employee is available during project timeline
  • Consider experience - Look at the employee's background and past projects
  • Approval workflow - Approved applications need final approval from Department Head
  • Provide feedback - Always provide constructive feedback for rejected applications

Tip: You can also manually assign employees from the "Assignments" page if needed.
